"Going There", a radio segment about travel ethics featured on Blugold Radio Sunday at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.

Andee Erickson co-hosts with Lara Bockenstedt every Sunday as they discuss different styles of travel and the tourist's role in those spaces and activities.

What exactly are we participating in as a tourist? This is a question they love to explore.
